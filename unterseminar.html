Junior Model Theory Seminar

This is the webpage of the Junior Model Theory Seminar at the University of Münster (Summer Semester 2026).

Everyone who is interested in model theory and does not hold a PhD yet is very welcome to attend the seminar and to possibly also give a talk. The aim is that we can get an idea on what the people around us are doing. The seminar is a safe space where you can also try out talks on ongoing work etc. It is a good opportunity to practice giving talks :-)

Since people are travelling a lot this semester, we have decided to do a learning seminar. But giving talks on their own research is still welcomed.

The seminar is taking place on Fridays at 11:00 a.m. in Weyl-Kammer on the first floor of Orléans-Ring 10. We meet there at 10:30 for tea.
